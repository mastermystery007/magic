## Recording standard

- Video: 1080p, 30 fps. Capture audience view, participant view and overhead handling where relevant.
- Macro shots: lock focus and exposure; use a neutral high-contrast background.
- Use only original or licensed music, illustrations, prop designs and scripts.
- Do not expose or reconstruct proprietary commercial gimmicks or branded routines.
- Use safe materials; no uncontrolled flame, hazardous chemicals, dangerous magnets, sharp objects or unsafe electrical methods.

## Content and attribution rules

Research may draw from historical magic and mentalism literature, but all app text, scripts, illustrations, prop artwork, diagrams and routine combinations must be original. Do not publish commercial gimmick construction, exact proprietary handling, copied scripts, scans, dealer instructions or reverse-engineered branded methods.

For general principles such as secret writing, billets, one-ahead, forces and indexes, add historical credit where reasonably known. Keep a private rights ledger containing asset creator, licence, source, purchase receipt where relevant and permitted use.

## Safety rules for production

- Thread and magnetic work must be tested around lighting, electronics and medical-device risk.
- Hidden-message materials must be washable, non-toxic and tested on the exact surface.
- No skin application unless the product is explicitly intended for cosmetic skin use and patch-tested by the participant.
- No uncontrolled flame or volatile chemicals.
- Participants must consent to filming and public release.
- Supernatural themes must remain clearly theatrical and must not create real threats, curses or spiritual authority.

## Release acceptance criteria

- All lessons, search, bookmarks, completion and quizzes work offline.
- Every media file has an original/licensed rights record.
- No missing-media controls remain enabled in the public release.
- All prop and material instructions pass a safety review.
- Three external test users can understand setup, performance and recovery without extra explanation.
- Android production build installs, resumes and preserves progress on a physical low-memory device.
